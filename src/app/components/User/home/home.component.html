About

The website offers a range of features and resources designed to support the architecture community. It provides a platform for architects to showcase their projects, share their design philosophies, and gain recognition for their work. Users can browse through a diverse collection of architectural designs, ranging from residential buildings to commercial complexes, and find inspiration for their own projects.

Create Community

Taking into account the social, economic and ecological context, we encourage dynamic, healthy communities through design. We master plan for a balance of shared and private space that invites conviviality. Community involvement in the process ensures that all members are heard and honored.The website also offers discussion forums and chat rooms where members can engage in lively conversations, seek advice, and collaborate on design challenges. These interactive features facilitate knowledge exchange and allow professionals and students to learn from each other's experiences.
